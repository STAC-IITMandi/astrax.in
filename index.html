What is AstraX and why visit IIT Mandi?

AstraX, the inter-college Astro-Meet of Indian Institute of Technology Mandi is the attraction center for students and professors with an inclination towards computational and theoretical astronomy.
Bringing together space science and technology, AstraX is the one and only of it's kind marked by the acing competitions, prodigious talks, lauded workshops and much more.
Surrounded by serene mountains and forests, IIT Mandi has a great night sky for astronomy enthusiasts where major constellations and the Milky Way Galaxy are visible with the naked eye. Located near the Uhl tributary, the institute has a green, pure and peaceful campus away from the city buzz.

Outreach Program

  • Workshop by Aryabhat Foundation.

    As a part of Corporate Social Responsibility, AstraX’20 is organizing a Basic Astronomy Workshop in collaboration with Aryabhat Foundation for middle school students of nearby schools. Aryabhat Foundation is a special endeavor for the promotion of basic sciences in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh. By organizing events like film shows and solar astronomy workshops in our outreach program, we aim to promote astronomy education in these students and thus develop an interest in astronomy and astrophysics among them, on a basic level.

  • Introductory Python Workshop

    AstraX’20 is also organizing a Python workshop for high school students. We aim at introducing the importance of python language and astronomy using python. This workshop also concentrates on various other topics like open source and closed source development in Python, its uses in places other than astronomy, and so much more. Mentors from IIT Mandi will help out these students to understand the basics programming using python language. We feel it is our responsibility to help local underprivileged students make a leap of faith and get a hands-on to programming in early stages of their life.
