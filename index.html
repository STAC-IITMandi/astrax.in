About AstraX

AstraX is the inter-college astronomy themed fest organised annually by the students of Indian Institute of Technology Mandi. Spanning multiple days and including several engaging events, talks & workshops, it is the centre of attraction for students and professors with an inclination towards computational and theoretical astronomy.

For Participants

Astrax '22 is coming soon! Details of the fest will be updated on this page as and when they are finalized. Do take a look at the activities of previous years' editions meanwhile.

The fest is being conducted online this year, between the 14th and 17th of April 2022.
