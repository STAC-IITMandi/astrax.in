About AstraX

AstraX is the inter-college astronomy themed fest organised annually by the students of Indian Institute of Technology Mandi. Spanning multiple days and including several engaging events, talks & workshops, it is the centre of attraction for students and professors with an inclination towards computational and theoretical astronomy.

For Participants

The fest is being conducted online this year, between 30th April 2022 and 3rd May 2022. Details will be updated on this page as and when they are finalized, along with registration links.

Activities in this year's edition of the fest include 6 events open to all registered participants, a School championship for students at the middle & high school levels, 2 workshops offering hands-on experience, and more !
